Face-to-Face Set-up

1. Attend the venue on time
2. Raise your hand if you want to ask or say something
3. Participate in our activities
4. Do not be noisy especially during lectures
5. Have Fun


Online Set-up

1. Make sure to open your cameras for it is required for some of our activities.
2. Keep your microphones mute at all times, unless you are asked to unmute it to speak.
3. Comment sections are for questions, concerns and activities purposes only.
4. Have Fun


DURATION :

The workshop will have a time scale of 4 hours. It will start from 10:00 AM (sharp) up until 2:00 PM. It is planned to be done on June 05, 2021. The time duration is based on the estimation of how much activity we plan to conduct and how much it will take a simple discussion. The team as well guarantees the snacks and/or lunch meals that the participants may have after the workshop if it is going to be executed face-to-face. Moreover, our online workshop will be held in a zoom meeting if we don’t get the chance to conduct it face-to-face.

VENUE :

We will assure everyone that the venue of our workshop will be able to satisfy the demand of space with reference to the activities that we plan to implement and the maximum total of our participants. Our venue will vary as our community starts to grow, we want them to be able to participate comfortably and no contamination; this will depend on our future sponsors/partnership, however for now, we will stick to our venue located in the 2nd floor of Chinabank building at Paseo De Roxas. The action regarding waste disposal that we plan to implement will be conducted in various places according to partnership and others with the power and space to let us conduct our workshop activities.

PARTICIPANTS :

The workshop and the team aims to have 15-30 participants. The target attendees will be students around our age or more likely teenagers/students who ages from 13 to 18 years old. The decision’s purpose is to encourage youth in making changes in the environment and improving the overall quality of the world's condition. These adolescents may help contribute to the future world’s pleasant ambiance.



OBJECTIVES :

Our objective is to help the planet by educating people concerning the importance of waste disposal. We want our participants to learn how to dispose wastes more effectively and not just throw it in trash bins without balancing the types of garbage. We also want to teach our beloved participants the significance of working together and understanding why some people recklessly throw trash everywhere. In summary, our objective is to help the planet by the power of knowledge and unity of the people.
