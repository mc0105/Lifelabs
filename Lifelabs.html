WORKSHOP OVERVIEW

The workshop also called “Together We Sanitize” is brought to you by Team 6. Our workshop is all about Solid Waste Management. Solid Waste Management helps lessen and prevent polution. In this workshop, we will teach you how to appropriately dispose your wastes, introduce the causes and effects of waste disposal, its importance and advantages and disadvantages. Our goal is to help the planet by the power of knowledge and unity of people. The workshop is arranged in order to save the earth from a plethora of consequences that is coming, especially that other wastes such as chemical and electronic wastes are increasingly ubiquitous in our society and it gives a large impact to our environment. We also proffer an appliance that can benefit both the environment and non-financial people to help resolve land pollution.

BENEFIT/S OF THE
WORKSHOP

It will benefit everyone since it will spread awareness and education with regard to the disadvantages of improper disposal of wastes. It will also open their minds to change their ways and be mindful of their actions for the better future.

IMPORTANCE OF THE
WORKSHOP

It is important in view of the fact that it will unite everyone to continue saving our mother earth by preventing people from littering. It will make them realize how their actions can affect the environment and it will also teach them how to dispose wastes properly.

PROPOSED SOLUTION

Our team came up with a solution called "BinBin Machine"; this is the solution that we thought of to help us prevent and lessen pollution that we are experiencing due to our daily wastes. We want to help the nature and this machine would help people learn how to separate trash (i.e., recyclable, non-biodegradable, biodegradable and electronic wastes). Ergo, even in that way people will learn to throw garbage properly. We came up with this solution by deciding and discussing if this would literally help our ecosystem and can reduce/prevent future problems.

BINBIN MACHINE

Our team plans to propose a product called “BinBin Machine”. It is similar to a vending machine, however it exchanges money for recyclable/reusable materials. The money that the machine exchanges will depend on how much their items are valued, for there will be a scanner in each category and a weighing machine. We also plan to include biodegradable wastes that will be distributed to farmers or to workers that may convert it to fertilizers. The machine’s rubbish will be collected (3) thrice a week and will get checked everyday regarding the amount of rubbish that has been collected and how much money is left. Maintenance will be implemented once a month to assure that our machine will work efficiently.
